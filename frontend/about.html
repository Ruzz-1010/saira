Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a premier luxury destination

Two Decades of Hospitality Excellence

Founded in 2000, GrandStay Hotel began as a small family-run establishment with just 20 rooms. Our founder, Miguel Santos, envisioned a place where travelers could experience true Filipino hospitality combined with world-class amenities.

Over the years, we've grown into a 5-star luxury hotel with 200 beautifully appointed rooms and suites, multiple dining options, and state-of-the-art facilities. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values: warm hospitality, attention to detail, and personalized service.

Today, GrandStay Hotel stands as a landmark of luxury in Manila, consistently ranked among the top hotels in the Philippines and receiving numerous international awards for our service and facilities.
